Sushi

In Japan's land of the rising sun,

Where culinary delights are second to none,

There's a dish that's known the world over,

A gastronomic treasure, it's called sushi, my lover.


From the sea it comes, the fish so fresh,

Wrapped in seaweed, a savory flesh,

Rice, vinegar, and sugar, the perfect blend,

A symphony of flavors, that never ends.


Maguro, hamachi, uni, and tako,

So many choices, it's hard to know,

A rainbow of colors, a feast for the eye,

Sushi, oh sushi, you never will die.


A master chef, with skills honed over years,

Creates a masterpiece, that brings happy tears,

With precision and care, each piece is made,

A work of art, on a plate it's displayed.


Wasabi and soy sauce, the perfect dip,

Savoring each bite, with a sigh on our lips,

In our mouths it melts, a party of taste,

An experience we'll never let go to waste.


With chopsticks in hand, we savor each piece,

Sushi, our love, will never decrease,

From Tokyo to New York, it's revered,

An international treasure, we hold it dear.


Sushi, oh sushi, you're more than a dish,

You're an expression of culture, a savory wish,

For those who love you, you're a part of our soul,

A culinary experience, that makes us whole.


So here's to sushi, a delight beyond measure,

A gift from the sea, a culinary treasure,

May it forever remain, our beloved dish,

Sushi, oh sushi, you're our every wish.
